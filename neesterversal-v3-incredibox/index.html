About Neesterversal v3 Incredibox

Neesterversal v3 is a special mode that expands the world of Incredibox. It features 20 different Neester characters, each with their own unique sounds and styles.

How to Play:

  • Use headphones for a better experience!
  • Drag and drop an icon on a character to have them sing.
  • Drag down a character to have them stop singing.

Welcome to the third edition of the Neesterversal, featuring 20 different Neesters from 20 different mods! There's some lore hidden in the game too, feel free to explore it!
